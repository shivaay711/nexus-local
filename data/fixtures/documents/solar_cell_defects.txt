CellScan Project Notes: Solar Cell Defect Detection

Electroluminescence (EL) imaging reveals defects in photovoltaic cells that
are invisible to the naked eye. Common defect classes include micro-cracks,
finger interruptions, and dark regions caused by broken busbars.

The CellScan pipeline fine-tunes a ResNet-18 backbone on EL images. Grad-CAM
heatmaps localize the image regions that drive each prediction, and
temperature scaling calibrates the model's confidence scores so that reported
probabilities match empirical accuracy.

Class imbalance is handled with weighted sampling. All metrics must come from
a real training run; the project forbids fabricated numbers in the README.
